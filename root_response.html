The Collaboration Journey

How Process Works

We follow an intimate design ritual to ensure your final physical commission represents a part of your soul.

01
Step 1

Consultation

Discuss your initial ideas, preferred canvas sizing, target placement, and dark luxury visual motifs directly with the head curator.

02
Step 2

Reference Collection

Send design references, mood boards, specific tattoo templates, or personal sketch ideas to map out the visual coordinates.

03
Step 3

Concept Development

Our studio lays out the digital template drafts, placement geometry, color maps, and creative guidelines for your approval.

04
Step 4

Production

Production begins. The textile is prepared, treated, and hand-painted/hand-distressed. This is the ritual where the wearable art is born.

05
Step 5

Finishing

Final detailing check. We dry-bake and thermally cure the pigments, stitch custom inner leather labels, and complete final structural testing.

06
Step 6

Delivery

Your 1:1 commission is safely enclosed in our branded black cedar cases and dispatched globally via high-security, trackable couriers.
